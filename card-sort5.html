Production Ready Django

Django is the framework that powers some of the world’s most popular websites and web applications, such as Instagram, Pinterest and Disqus.

Scaling Django, both in terms of features and users, is difficult. As with any framework, it is more balanced towards developer efficiency than performance and optimization. “Production Ready Django” will help you gain the skills you need to create robust applications that are ready to go live.
